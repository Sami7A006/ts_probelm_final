Hackathon Rules & Regulations

Sustainable Energy & Electric Mobility Innovation Hackathon — official guidelines, judging criteria, and code of conduct for all participants.

01

Hackathon Tracks

Track I – Sustainable Energy & Smart Solar Solutions
Participants develop innovative solutions related to renewable energy and solar technologies.

Smart Solar Energy Systems AI-Driven Solar Solutions Renewable Energy Innovations Solar Storage & Management

Track II – Electric Mobility & Green Transportation
Participants focus on technologies related to electric vehicles and sustainable transportation.

EV Innovations EV Charging Infrastructure Smart EV Ecosystems Sustainable Transport
02

General Rules

  1. Teams must select only one track for their project.
  2. All participants should bring their project components.
  3. All projects must be developed during the hackathon duration.
  4. Previously developed or submitted projects are not allowed.
  5. Projects must address a real-world problem related to the selected track.
  6. Participants must maintain professional and respectful behaviour throughout the event.
  7. Plagiarism or copying existing work will result in disqualification.
  8. College will ensure thorough security checks at any instant. Any unwanted material found will lead to strict action and disqualification.
  9. College reserves the right to restrict participant movement on campus at any time.
  10. The organizing committee reserves the right to disqualify any team found in violation of these guidelines. Final decisions regarding rules and evaluation are binding.
03

Code of Conduct

🤝

Respect all participants, mentors, and organizers at all times.

🚫

No harassment, discrimination, or abusive behaviour will be tolerated.

🚭

Alcohol, smoking, and drugs are strictly prohibited during the event.

🏫

Participants must follow all campus safety and security regulations.

💬

Engage in clear communication, teamwork, and creativity when presenting your solution.

✨

Operate with integrity, being accountable for your actions and supporting each other.

⚠ Failure to follow the code of conduct may result in immediate disqualification.

04

06

Facilities & Logistics

  • 🍽 Food will be provided at designated areas and scheduled times.
  • 📦 Registration kits will be distributed at the registration desk.
  • 🧹 Participants must maintain cleanliness in the workspace.
  • 🔒 The organizers are not responsible for personal belongings.

📌 IMPORTANT NOTE
By participating in the hackathon, all participants agree to follow the rules and guidelines mentioned in this rulebook. The decision of the organizing committee and judges will be final and binding.
